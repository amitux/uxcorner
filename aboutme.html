Hi there!

 

My name is Amit. I am a user experience designer working at Photon Interactive as Creative Lead for its UX practice.

 

Designs are meant to help societies evolve as a better place to live in. The mission of my life is to touch and transform the lives of more than one million people through design.

 

Being a user experience designer, I design with empathy for human needs which are meant for societies to help them evolve as a better place to live in.

At Photon, I am responsible for inseminating design thinking into execution of projects, along with the regular responsibilities as an interaction designer. I evangelise user experience, its processes and its power to transform the world.

 

Prior to Photon, I have worked in Infosys as Interaction Designer for nearly four years, where I have accomplished more than 20 projects involving interaction design, visual design, user research, concept building, and interface evaluation.
