About The Project

 

This app is part of an initiative of the educational wing of one of the world's largest manufacturer of micro chips and processors.

It helps students learn how to construct well reasoned arguments and prove their case with credible evidences.

The Challenge

 

• Replicating the desktop interface into mobile device with keeping almost all the functionalities available

• Making it easy -to-use for kids

• Making it consistent with other existing apps on the same platform yet trendy

 

Solution Approach

 

A detailed content audit and information architecture helped in prioritising the tasks to make it easier for kids.

The app was also evaluated by the real users and design was iterated to improve on some of the identified bottlenecks. This was set as a model project following new full-fledged UX process with a separate UX Specification standard.

Heuristic Evaluation

Wireframe Design & Technical Feasibility Review

 

 

Wireframes were designed using visio, and got it reviewed by the technical team. The iterative model of wireframe design refined the wireframes to make it easier to develop as well.

A content audit and the outcomes of heurisitc evaluation on the basis of content strategy gave a foundation to new IA. New flow model helped in restructuring of the information architecture for the mobile app.

Visual Design, Styleguides & Assets

 

 

Wireframes were translated into visual design using Photoshop based on the style guidelines provided by the customer. Style specification document was created along with the design assets to deliver it to the development team.
