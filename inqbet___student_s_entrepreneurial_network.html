About The Project

 

The idea for Inqbet was conceptualised with an objective to understand the Design process right from the bottom strategy level up to Visual Design step.

It's a concept of such a system that could promote and nurture fresh potential ideas from college campuses and invite investors and mentors to help the students pursuing their dream.

The Challenge

 

• Coming up with a fresh concept and designing it from strategy level up to visual design

• Designing different models for the whole system spun around it

• Creating branding guidelines and following them

 

 

Solution Approach

 

Brainstorming followed by field research, creating personas, scenarios, storyboards, contextual design models, information architecture, wireframe design and  visual design was done as part of solution for this project.

Strategy & Scope

 

Inqbet, starts with bright, energetic people with great ideas, integrates all the help and support the idea conceivers need to build and nurture their ideas to realise it into a great startup. This exercise defined what, why and how of the project. Initially it was outlined on a large chartsheet, and later it was defined in the form of multiple documents.

Solution

We did user research and profiles as well as personas were created based on the behavioral attributes and their other goals,pain-points & expectations.

Scnearios were written for the personas and storyboards were done to have a clear understnading of users.

Analysis

 

 

Further, the system was analysed and different work- models were prepared.

Sequence model, flow model, cultural model, artifact model & physical models were created to have a clear understanding of the whole ecosystem and the experience which could be created.

Wireframe & Visual Design

 

 

Initially low fidelity wireframes were created in balsamiq and later it was refined to high fidelity following 960 grid format using Visio tool. Visual design was done using Illustrator after finalizing on branding styleguides.

Solution

 

The final solution comprises of wireframes, visual design and style assets.
