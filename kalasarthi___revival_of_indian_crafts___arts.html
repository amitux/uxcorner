About The Project

 

KalaSarthi symbolises the force which drives the arts and crafts in rural India. With the objective of providing a sustainable livelihood to the rural artisans, KalaSarthi was born with a mission to take the Indian crafts market to a different level.

The Challenge

 

 

• Conceptualisation of the idea

• Research on Indian Crafts Market

• Strategy & Scope definition

 

 

 

 

Solution Approach

 

Heuristic Evaluation from the perspective of all five layers- Strategy, Scope, Structure, Skeleton & Surface was done. It gave useful insights for the scope of improvement in their prioritized order. Other strategies were also thought through to promote the online experience. Further, scenarios were defined , storyboarding and restructuring of information architecture was done, which was much needed. It followed by wireframes and visual design with the existing branding guidelines.

Understanding Users & Context

 

After surveying and conducting user research , we were able to understand the painpoints of users like - Information overload, difficult find ability, navigation, unintuitive content, overall unstructured information hierarchy, no error prevention & recovery options, untidy interface with no aesthetics.
