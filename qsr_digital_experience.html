About The Project

 

Getting food easily in no time : This is an app to function on kiosk in QSRs and mobile phones to order food, pay and get the food in minimal time. The project also included ideas for creating experiences at QSR an enjoying experience as a whole.

The Challenge

 

• Developing the whole end to end experience from booking an order from app till continuing the order and delivery at the store

• Conceptualising the scenarios and solidifying the product concept further

• Communicating the right concept to the team through sketches and LFWs

• Designing interactions for kiosk as a different and unique form factor.

 

 

Solution Approach

 

The idea was brainstormed among the team members and the flow model was prepared. The four easy steps were identified to order the food. The key differentiators were to provide contextual menu ready for quick navigation and verify the order momentarily.

The delivery of this design was made possible in merely two working days and through collaborative design and a team work.
