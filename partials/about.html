Amit Kumar Tiwari | User Experience Designer

Amit is a UX Designer at HPE, and currently designing digital products for new-age datacenters. He has been designing solutions and digital experiences for more than 7 years for brands like Apple, Boeing, Goldman Sachs and Microsoft to name a few.

He also runs UX2DAY start-up friendly design group, which works closely with start-ups to improve and transform their customer experiences.
Every morning run brings him the energy for the day. Apart from being a fitness fan, he is an amateur artist, illustrator and a photography enthusiast.
