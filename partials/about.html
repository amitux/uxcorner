Amit Kumar Tiwari | User Experience Designer

Amit is a user experience designer. His designs empathise with human needs and are meant for societies to help them evolve as a better place to live in. He talks about - 3Ps of UX Design - Principles, Processes & Practices.
He enjoys spreading colors on canvas and fascinated towards street, portrait & macro photography. Amit thinks that frugal innovation can bring happiness for the impoverished world. So, he keeps experimenting with new ideas.
Amit is responsible for inseminating design thinking into execution of projects, along with his regular responsibilities as an interaction design engineer at Hewlett Packard Enterprise, Bangalore India. He is evangelising user experience, its processes and its power to transform the world. He firmly believes that - "Design has the solution of all the prevalent problems of the world"
