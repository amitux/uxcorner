| Websites & Digital Experiences for a Publishing Giant

About the customer

 

This project was for one of the world's largest publishers, distributors of children's books and a leader in educational technology and children's media. In addition to some most popular book series, the company is known for its school book clubs, book fairs and classroom magazines, They also publish educational technology programs and create digital learning programs.

User Experience : A Key Differentiator

• Site Content

• Browsing Paths

| Transforming Travel Experience : routePlanner

Project Brief

 

Its a very common situation among most of the travellers, who could not plan their travel in advance for some reason and end up with unavailability of their preferred mode/route of travel.

Makemytrip facilitates this through route planner with a single end-to-end trip plan via different modes of travel and different alternate routes as per your choice and preferences.

Reimagining travel experience as a gateway to happyness & comfort

 

Its not about just booking... but truely making your trip as pleasurable as possible. This is the story of designing routeplanner, which starts with research, followed by analysis, design, evaluation and prototype.

The foundation of a robust design lies in research, in the interest of timelines and stighter timelines, the research has been kept limited to -

- Understanding the users

- Understanding the context

Online questionaire survey has been used for user research and Comparative analysis has been done to understand the market well and introduce new concepts.

The detailed report on research findings can be accessed here -

http://amit-uxd.webs.com/userresearch_mmt-1.pdf

 

 

 

• Everything in single view : minimizes cognitive load, unnecessary inputs

• A detailed information panel for each route option right on the same screen with a call to action available right there. (Increased findability, readability and clear visual hierarchy)

• Interactive map facilitates in creating your customized route - Drag the route through a nearby city of your choice, get someone along with you from there or stay there for sometime. (All possible customizations and preferences are taken care of)

• The design follows the principles of material design and it brings in a fresh design through its appearance and interaction patterns for users after an era of Completely Flat Designs.

Converted research data into Persona, and quickly sketched out the navigation model for the app.

The simplest posssible navigation model was prepared with four basic steps -

Search for route -> Route Options -> View Route Details/Manipulate routes  ->  Booking

| Quick Service Restaurant Kiosk & Mobile App

GETTING FOOD EASILY IN NO TIME

 

This is an app to function on kiosk in QSRs and mobile phones to order food, pay and get the food in minimal time.

 

 

SOLUTION

 

The four easy steps were identified to order the food. The key differentiators were to provide contextual menu ready for quick navigation and verify the order momentarily.

 

The delivery of this design was made possible in merely two working days and through collaborative design and a team work.

 

THE CHALLENGE

 

Eliminating the unnecessary elements from design and the whole interaction in order to get the minimal and easy-to-use app was a challenge. This was successfully tackled by a collaborative design approach, in which multiple solutions were thought by participants and the best feasible solution was chosen.
