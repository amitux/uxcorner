First Do No Harm

First Do No Harm – Following the commandment can drastically change the experiences with more empathy and making the lives happier.Let’s see how a trivial ‘*’ changes the whole scene. It is a small design decision that fields which are not required should not be shown at first hand, but if still required it should be disabled without any mandatory tag on it.

Services

We strive to build awesome experiences for our customers every second. As offerings, we help throughout the ecosystem by defining design strategy, setting up the design framework as per customer needs and enabling our partners to offer the best experience for their end-customers.

Digital Design Startegy

Design Research

We conduct user and market research activities to uncover the realities and bring out the true picture of user's needs. It also evaluates the coolest ideas and their market potential through number of research methodologies.

Interaction Design Services

We design interactive prototypes with immaculate interface controls, delightful subtle transitions and minimalstic design techniques.

UX Consulting

The most popular offering is the complete UX solution delivery through iterative approach of human-centered-design and design thinking processes.

Corporate Design Learning

We have started design learning curriculum for designers, design-aspirants and professionals, based on hands-on learning of well-established theories and principles of design. The emphasis always stays on learning contemporary design practices through real time projects.
