| Pragmatic UX for Service Industry

Similar to quick service restuarants, IT service sector has been focusing on quick and demand based services. It’s been talked a lot whether IT services should focus on the usability and the overall user experience of the product for end-users.

The relevance of UX is well understood and realized easily in case of product based companies, because its very clearly communicated whether something works or not. However, in service industry its not always communicated back whether something really worked or not.

 

So, to understand the whole ecosystem and facilitate a solution for such situations ‘Pragmatic UX for Services’ was born. It incorporates the follwoing characteristics and ensures the better quality of the product with guaranteed value from UX perspective.

 

- Quick & Iterative

- Collaborative in Nature (Virtual Collaboration can also be encouraged)

- Process - Oriented

- As per Contemporary Standards & Practices

 

Pragmatic UX for Services is a framework with set of activities which defines the design solution plan beforehand and drive the process quickly in collaboration with other teams. It follows the principles of  Ideate - Narrow down ideas on validation - Build.

 

The set of activities for different phases of design process has been well curated to be accomplished faster and stay focussed towards the context of design.

These activities are innovative, new and fun-filed to create innovative patterns and ideas.

| Showing Evidence Learning App

The activities performed in this project are -

• Heuristics Evaluation of the web app

• Information Architecture for the mobile app

• Brainstorming for the navigational flow

• Wireframes Designing for mobile and tab.

• Iterative Design of Wireframes

• Visual designs for the key screens

• Icons & Assets designing

• Style guides

THE CHALLENGE

 

Replicating the desktop interface into mobile device with keeping almost all the functionalities available and making it easy -to-use for kids were the key challenges.

A detailed content audit and information architecture helped in prioritizing the tasks to make it easier for kids.

 

PROJECT BRIEF

This app is an initiative of the educational wing of one of the largest manufacturer of PC and micro chips in the world.

It helps students learn how to construct well reasoned arguments and prove their case with credible evidences.

The app was also evaluated by the real users and design was iterated to improve on some of the identified bottlenecks.
