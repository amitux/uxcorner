ABOUT THE PROJECT

 

Its a very common situation among most of the travellers, who could not plan their travel in advance for some reason and end up with unavailability of their preferred mode/route of travel. Makemytrip facilitates this through routeplanner with a single end-to-end trip plan via different modes of travel and different alternate routes as per your choice and preferences.

THE CHALLENGE 

 

Conceptualisation of the routeplanner so as to work best in Indian context

Should be optimised for web and mobile devices

Presenting all relevant information on differently sized devices

Consistent with the other applications and their interactions

 

SOLUTION APPROACH

 

A 5-stage design route was followed to design the solution.

Design Solution

RESEARCH

 

The foundation of a robust design lies in research, in the interest of timelines and stighter timelines, the research has been kept limited to -

- Understanding the users

- Understanding the context

 

Online questionnaire survey was done for user research and Comparative analysis was done to understand the market well and introduce new concepts. The detailed report on research findings can be accessed here.

 

 

ANALYSIS & DESIGN

 

Converted research data into Persona, and quickly sketched out the navigation model for the app. The simplest possible navigation model was prepared with four basic steps -

 

Search for route -> Route Options -> View Route Details/Manipulate routes  ->  Booking 

 

A sketch concept on paper was refined in the form of interactive wireframes created in Axure Pro 7.  The interactive demo can be found here.

 

 

DESIGN PROTOTYPES : USPs of the Design Solution

 

• Everything in single view : minimises cognitive load, unnecessary inputs

 

• A detailed information panel for each route option right on the same screen with a call to action available right there. (Increased find-ability, readability and clear visual hierarchy)

 

• Interactive map facilitates in creating your customised route - Drag the route through a nearby city of your choice, get someone along with you from there or stay there for sometime. (All possible customisations and preferences are taken care of)

 

• The design follows the principles of material design and it brings in a fresh design through its appearance and interaction patterns for users after an era of Completely Flat Designs.
