1. DESIGN DRILLS (1000 - 1100 hrs)

The Camp starts with few drills, which would make us ponder. ...and we'll play with set of design problems and relevant kits. Get your hands dirty there!

2. APPRECIATE DESIGN (1100 - 1130 hrs)

Appreciate good designs and think about bad ones too. Observe the experiences and talk about them, because it's all about the experience.

3. NET - BREAK (1130 - 1140 hrs)

It's all about coffee and gossips. .. and yes Network!

4. HCD - WORKSHOP (1140 - 1240 hrs)

An interactive discussion on the creative method of problem solving, that is Human-Centered-Design.
The discussion would be extended to the workshop focused on HCD process.

5. UXD SCHOOL DEMO (1240 - 1300 hrs)

Let your career fly higher with sharper design skills. UXD school offers corporate design learning programmes in user experience design for career development.
The team would present a short demo and their structure.
